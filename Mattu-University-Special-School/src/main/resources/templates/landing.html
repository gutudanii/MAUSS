BACKGROUND

The Mattu University Special School (MaUSS), founded in 2013 (E.C.) and expanded in 2020 (G.C.). MaUSS operates under Mattu University, providing specialized education for students in grades 9-12. The school's commitment to academic excellence is evident in its strict entrance exams, where students are selected based on their performance in the 8th-grade school or ministry exams. Since its establishment with an initial enrollment of 41 students, MaUSS has grown, currently accommodating 141 students. This growth is a testament to the school's dedication to bring up top-tier talent and promote a learning environment where students thrive.
